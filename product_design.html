Product Design @ Hy-Vee

Due to confidentiality requirements, I'm unable to share most of my design work from Hy-Vee. If you'd like to discuss my experience further, please contact me.

I'm the lead designer for Hy-Vee's new Aisles Online mobile app (iOS/Android). I'm responsible for UX research, customer flows, wireframes, UI/visual design, motion design and user testing. I also collaborate with engineering teammates to implement a design system and reusable/scalable React Native components for the platform. Occasionally, I even contribute front-end code (React Native) when needed.

I previously designed and tested Hy-Vee's food service self-serve kiosk as part of two-designer team. We synthesized business and customer requirements and delivered flows, wireframes and hi-fidelity visual designs on a very tight timeline. I conducted in-person user testing with customers and helped mobile team quickly iterate on and deploy design enhancements after launch. The kiosk is currently deployed in stores across the midwest.
