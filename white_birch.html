White Birch Resort

Mobile-friendly site mockup for White Birch Resort in Minnesota. The idea was to overhaul the existing site and make it more readable, accessible and responsive for users. The mockup includes call to action buttons, an amenities-finder "app", Google Map, and an email sign up form. The site is built with HTML and CSS, as well as a tiny bit of Javascript (written well before I had any idea what I was doing with JS!)
