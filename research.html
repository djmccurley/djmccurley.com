Research

Due to confidentiality requirements, I'm unable to share most of my UX research. If you'd like to discuss my experience further, please contact me.

As a skilled UX researcher, I've conducted qualitative and quantitative studies to deliver memorable customer experiences and outstanding business value. These include informal customer observation and interviews, long-form moderated UX testing, asynchronous online testing (Chalkmark, surveys, etc) and quick-hitting guerrilla UX studies.

In a past life, I also conducted and published graduate-level research in Hispanic Linguistics and child language acquisition. While at The Ohio State University, I researched syntax & semantics, sociolinguistics and child language development, and I conducted field research in Mexico City.
